### URL & metadata conventions (added — needed for dev implementation)
- Slugs: lowercase, hyphenated, keyword-matched (e.g. `/solutions/website-development`, `/industries/healthcare`, `/insights/what-is-growth-engineering`)
- Title tag template: `{Primary Keyword} | Infynix — Growth Engineering Company` (≤60 characters)
- Meta description: 150–160 characters, written per page, includes target keyword + CTA verb
- One canonical URL per page; avoid duplicate content across Solutions/Industries combinations

---

## TECHNICAL SEO REQUIREMENTS

- Semantic HTML5, single H1 per page, logical heading hierarchy
- Schema.org markup: Organization (sitewide), Service (per Solutions page), FAQPage (every page with an FAQ block), BreadcrumbList (all interior pages), Article (every Insights post), LocalBusiness (Contact page)
- XML Sitemap, robots.txt, canonical URLs
- Open Graph + Twitter Card metadata per page
- Internal linking strategy: every Solutions page links to relevant Industries and Case Studies; every Insights article links back to its parent Solutions/Growth Engineering page
- Core Web Vitals optimization (LCP, CLS, INP), image optimization (WebP/AVIF with fallback), lazy loading below the fold
- Keyword-friendly URLs, accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Redirect map: 301-redirect all existing indexed URLs to their new equivalents to preserve link equity (critical since navigation/IA is changing — flag this explicitly to the developer before launch)
- Analytics/tracking: GA4 + Meta Pixel installed sitewide before launch, with goal/event tracking on both CTAs (Discovery Call form submit, WhatsApp click) so lead attribution works from day one

---

## CONTENT WRITING GUIDELINES

**Tone:** professional, consultative, technical, business-focused, educational

**Avoid:** generic agency buzzwords, keyword stuffing, empty superlatives ("best," "leading," "world-class") without evidence, overly promotional language

Write for decision-makers: lead with business value, follow with the technology or service that enables it.
